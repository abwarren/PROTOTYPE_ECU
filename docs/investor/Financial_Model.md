## Key Drivers

- Hardware margin improves with volume (10-15% improvement at 1,000+ units)
- Subscription margin is very high (90%+ once cloud infrastructure scales)
- Marketplace is pure margin after platform built
- OEM licensing is high-margin, long sales cycle
