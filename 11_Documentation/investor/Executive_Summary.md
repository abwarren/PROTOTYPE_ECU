# Executive Summary

**Prototype ECU** is building the next generation of standalone engine control units (ECUs) for the automotive performance market.

We combine proven, production-tested engine control firmware with a modern, cloud-connected desktop application — delivering a premium tuning experience at a fraction of the cost of established competitors.

---

## The Opportunity

The automotive performance ECU market is estimated at **$500M+ annually** and is dominated by aging products with outdated user interfaces. The leading competitor (Motec, ~$3,000-8,000 per ECU) hasn't meaningfully changed its software in a decade. Meanwhile, the number of performance vehicles — both ICE and hybrid — continues to grow.

Workshops and tuners are demanding:
- Modern, intuitive tuning software
- Cloud connectivity for remote diagnostics
- Mobile apps for on-the-go monitoring
- Lower cost of entry
- Better data analysis tools

Existing solutions deliver on price OR quality. None deliver both.

## Our Solution

**Prototype Studio** — a premium desktop application that sets a new standard for ECU tuning software.

It looks nothing like existing ECU software. We've designed it to feel more like modern creative tools (Lightroom, Unreal Engine) than the grey Windows applications that dominate the market.

Behind the scenes, the firmware is based on rusEFI — the most technically advanced open-source ECU project — giving us a proven, stable engine control foundation without years of R&D.

## Business Model

- **Hardware:** ECU priced at $500-1,500 (vs $3,000+ for competitors)
- **Software:** Freemium Studio with paid cloud subscription
- **Revenue streams:** Hardware margin + SaaS subscriptions + calibration marketplace

## Team & Status

- Phase 1 foundation complete
- rusEFI firmware fork established
- Architecture documented
- DDD engineering policy adopted
- Ready for Phase 2: Studio development

## Investment Thesis

We're not trying to out-engineer the competition on firmware. We're competing on **user experience** — the part of the product customers interact with 95% of the time. Our path to revenue is faster and less risky than building a full ECU from scratch.

**Ask:** Seed round to fund Studio development, hardware prototyping, and initial manufacturing.
