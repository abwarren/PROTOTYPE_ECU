# Market Analysis

## Market Size

The standalone ECU market is a segment of the broader automotive performance and aftermarket ECU industry.

### TAM (Total Addressable Market)
**$2.5B+** — Global automotive performance ECU and engine management market, including hardware, software, and services.

### SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)
**$500M+** — Standalone aftermarket ECUs for performance vehicles, including:
- Enthusiast builds (project cars, track cars)
- Professional workshops (tuning shops, motorsport)
- Specialty vehicles (kit cars, EV conversions, resto-mods)

### Target Segments (V1-V2)

| Segment | Size | Price Sensitivity | Willingness to Switch |
|---------|------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Enthusiast builder | Large | High | High (priced out of premium) |
| Independent workshop | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| Motorsport team | Small | Low | Low (entrenched) |
| Specialty vehicle | Growing | Medium | High |

## Market Trends

### Tailwinds

1. **Growing performance market** — More discretionary spending on vehicles post-pandemic
2. **EV conversions** — New demand for standalone controllers for electric drivetrains
3. **Aging demographics of tuners** — Younger generation expects modern software
4. **Open-source acceptance** — rusEFI, Speeduino, and others have legitimized the technology
5. **Cloud infrastructure maturity** — AWS/Azure have made IoT accessible for small hardware companies
6. **Workshop consolidation** — Larger workshops need fleet management and remote capabilities

### Competitive Landscape

**Premium Tier ($3,000-$8,000)**
- MoTeC — Market leader, aging software, premium pricing
- Haltech — Strong in motorsport, limited cloud
- ECU Master — Value-oriented, limited software innovation

**Mid Tier ($1,000-$3,000)**
- AEM — Good hardware, proprietary software ecosystem
- Link ECU — Strong in motorsport, niche following
- Holley / Terminator — Dominant in US V8 market

**Budget Tier ($200-$800)**
- Megasquirt — DIY-focused, aging software
- Speeduino — Open-source, limited polish
- rusEFI — Open-source, best technology but GPL-licensed

### How We Compete

| Tier | Competitor | Our Advantage |
|------|------------|---------------|
| Premium | MoTeC, Haltech | 1/3-1/2 the price, modern software, cloud features |
| Mid | AEM, Link ECU | Better software, cloud, mobile app |

## Go-to-Market Strategy

1. **Enthusiast first** — Target the underserved enthusiast market that's priced out of premium ECUs
2. **Workshop adoption** — Enthusiasts become professionals; bring their preferred tool with them
3. **Fleet and OEM** — White-label platform for commercial applications
