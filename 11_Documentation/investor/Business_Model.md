# Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Hardware Sales (Primary — Initial)

The ECU itself is the entry point. We sell a professionally packaged, fully featured standalone ECU at a competitive price.

| Stream | Product | Target Price | Margin | Notes |
|--------|---------|--------------|--------|-------|
| ECU (Base) | 8 injector + 8 ignition + ETC | $499-799 | 40-50% | Entry-level |
| ECU (Pro) | 16 injector + 16 ignition + ETC + cellular | $999-1,499 | 45-55% | Professional |
| Accessories | Harness, sensors, adapters | $50-300 | 30-50% | Ecosystem |

### 2. Software Subscriptions (Recurring — Growth)

Studio is free with basic features. Premium features require a subscription.

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Studio Free | $0 | Dashboard, basic tuning, local datalogging |
| Studio Pro | $15/mo or $150/yr | Auto-tuning, advanced analysis, plugin access |
| Cloud Connect | $25/mo | Telemetry, remote diagnostics, OTA, mobile app |
| Fleet | $99/mo | Multi-vehicle dashboard, fleet telemetry, priority support |

**Target:** 30% of hardware customers convert to paid subscription within 6 months.

### 3. Marketplace (Emerging — Phase 2+)

| Stream | Description | Revenue Share |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| Calibration marketplace | User-created engine calibrations | 20% commission |
| Plugin marketplace | Third-party plugins for Studio | 30% commission |
| UI themes | Custom dashboard themes | 30% commission |

### 4. OEM Licensing (Future — Phase 6)

| Stream | Description | Revenue |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| White-label license | Per-unit license for OEMs | $5-50/unit |
| Custom development | Branded variants for enterprise | Project-based |

## Revenue Model Summary

```
Year 1 (Launch Year):
  Hardware:          80% of revenue
  Subscriptions:     15% of revenue
  Marketplace:        5% of revenue

Year 3 (Growth):
  Hardware:          45% of revenue
  Subscriptions:     40% of revenue
  Marketplace:       15% of revenue

Year 5 (Scale):
  Hardware:          25% of revenue
  Subscriptions:     45% of revenue
  Marketplace:       20% of revenue
  OEM Licensing:     10% of revenue
```

## Unit Economics

| Metric | Estimate |
|--------|----------|
| ECU unit cost (COGS) | $250-400 (varies with volume) |
| ECU average selling price | $699 |
| Gross margin per ECU | 43-64% |
| Customer acquisition cost | $50-150 (affiliate, content, events) |
| Customer lifetime value | $800-2,500 (hardware + 2-3 years subscription) |
| Payback period | 3-6 months |
