## Enclosure Manufacturing

### Injection Molding (Plastic)
- **Materials:** PA66 (Nylon), PBT, ABS, PC
- **Tooling cost:** $5k-50k
- **MOQ:** 1000+ units
- **Automotive:** Glass-filled nylon for under-hood

### Die Casting (Aluminum)
- **Alloys:** ADC12, A380, 6061
- **Tooling cost:** $10k-80k
- **MOQ:** 500+ units

### Stamped Aluminum
- **Advantages:** Lower tooling cost, better heat transfer
- **MOQ:** 5000+ units

## TEN8 Manufacturing Roadmap

1. **Prototype** (1-100 units): PCBWay/JLCPCB + manual assembly
2. **Low volume** (100-1000 units): Kinwong or similar IATF supplier
3. **Medium volume** (1000-10000): Full production line with testing
4. **High volume** (10000+): Automated SMT, ICT, functional test
