# NXP S32K — MCU Research

## Overview

NXP's S32K family is designed specifically for automotive general-purpose and domain control applications.

## S32K1 Series

- **Core:** ARM Cortex-M4F, up to 112 MHz
- **Safety:** ASIL-B
- **Security:** CSEc (Cryptographic Service Engine)
- **CAN:** FlexCAN (CAN 2.0)
- **Temp:** -40°C to +125°C
- **Package:** LQFP, QFN variants

## S32K3 Series

- **Core:** ARM Cortex-M7, single/dual/lockstep, up to 320 MHz
- **Safety:** ASIL-D
- **Security:** HSM (EVITA Full)
- **CAN:** CAN FD, FlexRay, Ethernet
- **Timers:** eTPU, FTM, PIT, STM
- **Temp:** -40°C to +125°C/-150°C
- **Ecosystem:** S32 Design Studio, RTD (AUTOSAR)
