# Infineon AURIX — MCU Research

## Overview

AURIX is the industry standard for safety-critical automotive applications, particularly powertrain and ADAS.

## AURIX TC2xx

- **Core:** TriCore 1.6P, up to 200 MHz
- **Safety:** ASIL-D
- **Security:** HSM

## AURIX TC3xx

- **Core:** TriCore 1.6.2, up to 6 cores @ 300 MHz
- **Safety:** ASIL-D (lockstep cores)
- **Security:** HSM Full EVITA
- **Timers:** GTM (Generic Timer Module)
- **CAN:** CAN FD, FlexRay

## AURIX TC4xx

- **Core:** Next-gen TriCore, AI acceleration
- **Safety:** ASIL-D
- **New features:** CNN加速器, Ethernet 1G
