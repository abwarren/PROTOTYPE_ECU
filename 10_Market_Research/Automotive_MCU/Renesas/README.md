# Renesas RH850 — MCU Research

## Overview

RH850 series is widely used by Japanese OEMs in zonal and powertrain applications.

## Key Specs

- **Core:** Proprietary G4MH, single/multi-core
- **Safety:** ASIL-D
- **Security:** HSM (advanced)
- **CAN:** CAN FD, FlexRay, Ethernet
- **Timers:** TAUD, TSG

- e² studio, CS+

## TEN8 Applicability

**Secondary option.** Strong safety credentials but proprietary core adds tooling and ecosystem complexity. Better suited once TEN8 is established.
