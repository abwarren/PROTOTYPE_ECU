# Commercial ECU Platforms — Competitive Research

## Overview

Analysis of commercially available (proprietary) aftermarket ECU platforms for competitive benchmarking. These are not open-source but are relevant as they compete in the same market space as TEN8.

## Platforms Researched

| Platform | Origin | Market | Price Range | CAN | Mobile | Cloud |
|----------|--------|--------|-------------|-----|--------|-------|
| **Dicktator** | South Africa | Local (ZA) | ~$230 | No | No | No |
| **Spitronics** | South Africa | Regional (ZA/Africa) | ~$150-500 | No | No | No |

## Key Takeaways for TEN8

- **South Africa** is a strong, underserved market — both Dicktator and Spitronics dominate there but are technologically limited (no CAN, no mobile, no cloud)
- TEN8 with modern features (CAN FD, BLE mobile, cloud) could significantly disrupt this regional market
- Dicktator and Spitronics prove there's strong demand for affordable, locally-supported ECUs outside the US/Europe
